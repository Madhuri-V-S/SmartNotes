World War I

The Great War was a global conflict from 1914 to 1918 involving most of the world's great powers.

Imperialism is the policy of extending a nation's power through military force and colonization.

Nationalism: extreme pride in one's nation, often leading to rivalry and conflict with other nations.

The Treaty of Versailles was the peace agreement that officially ended World War I in 1919.

Trench warfare was a type of combat where opposing armies fought from fortified ditches.

The Allied Powers were the coalition including Britain, France, Russia, and later the United States.

Armistice is an agreement to stop fighting, used to end World War I on November 11, 1918.
